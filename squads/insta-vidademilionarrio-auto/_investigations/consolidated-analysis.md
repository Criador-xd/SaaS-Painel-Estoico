## Universal Patterns

### Structural Patterns
- **The "Context Shift"**: Starting with a visual hook that seems unrelated to the caption (e.g., a quote about peace paired with facts about the 80s). This increases "dwell time" as users read the unexpected info.
- **Narrative Carousels**: Using carousels to tell a story (either through dialogue or lifestyle snippets) rather than just listing 10 flat tips.

### Language Patterns
- **High-Impact Hooks**: Every top-performing post starts with a line that either challenges a common belief ("A gente corre tanto...") or validates a feeling ("Às vezes sua paz...").
- **Minimalist Copy**: Status is signaled through fewer words and lowercase formatting in lifestyle posts.

### Engagement Drivers
- **Keyword CTAs (Comenta X)**: The single most effective driver for comments.
- **Save-Bait Trivia**: Long captions with nostalgic facts that people save to "read later" or share with friends.

## Recommended Framework: "The 3 Pillars of Wealth Content"

This synthesized framework will guide the "Vida de Milionário" squad:

### 1. The Opening/Hook (Slide 1/Visual)
- **Constraint**: Must be a "Pattern Interrupt".
- **Patterns**: 
  - *The Paradox*: "Luxos que esquecemos que são luxos."
  - *The Call-out*: "Você tá certo. Errado sou eu..."
  - *The Status Signal*: A high-end asset (Porsche/Ferrari) paired with a gratitude line.

### 2. The Body Structure (Slides 2-9 / Caption)
- **Rotation**:
  - **Educational Days**: Use the `mentalidade_autoritaria` dialogue format (Scene -> Struggle -> Solution).
  - **Lifestyle Days**: Use the `eomavy` carousel format (Style -> Asset -> Purpose/Family).
  - **Viral Days**: Use the `soziinhooo` quote + trivia format.
- **Density**: Low visual density (few words per slide). High value in the caption (8-10 items/facts).

### 3. The Closing/CTA (Slide 10 / End of Caption)
- **Standard**: Keyword-trigger CTA ("Comenta MILIONARIO").
- **Secondary**: Shared-value CTA ("Envie para quem precisa ver isso").

## Hook Templates
1. **[The Paradox]**: "{Item} que as pessoas acham que é {A}, mas na verdade é {B}."
2. **[The Permission]**: "Sua {valor} vai te custar {preço} e está tudo bem."
3. **[The Reset]**: "Se nada mudar, {consequência}. Comente {KEYWORD} para o nosso Desafio."

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER DO)
1. **Generic Layouts**: Plain white backgrounds with black text only (looks too "educational/cheap").
2. **Long Slides**: More than 15 words per slide.
3. **Weak CTAs**: Just saying "Gostou? Me segue". Must provide a clear action.
4. **Missing Human Element**: Only showing money without the "Purpose" (Family/Peace).
