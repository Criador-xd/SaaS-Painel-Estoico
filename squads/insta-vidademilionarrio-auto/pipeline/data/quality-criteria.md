# Quality Criteria: Elite Standards

## 1. Content Scoring
| Criterion | Threshold | Why it matters |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| **Hook Velocity** | High (0-2s) | Determines if the user stops the scroll. |
| **Trust Score** | High | Avoids "Scammy" or "Mkt Digital" clichés. |
| **Aspiration Factor**| Very High | Must make the user want to enter that world. |
| **Clarity** | High | One core idea per post. |
| **Call to Action** | Mandatory | No post leaves without a clear command. |

## 2. Visual Benchmarks
- **Typography Palette**: Alliterative pairing of Playfair Display and Inter.
- **Negative Space**: Minimum 20% of the slide area must be empty.
- **Contrast**: WCAG AA (4.5:1) for all critical text.
- **Hierarchy**: Primary hook must be at least 1.5x larger than body text.

## 3. Brand Voice Checklist
- [ ] Tone is authoritative and direct (no "hedging").
- [ ] No academic or corporate jargon.
- [ ] Selective capitalized emphasis for power words.
- [ ] Consistent use of "Empire Gold" (#C5A059) for highlights.
- [ ] Footer @vidademilionarrio is present and legible.
